Start your Journey Now

Author : Renjith Ithikkat

check out and make your journey to Lake Louise via the Icefields Parkway, Canada's most spectacular mountain highway with views of glaciers, lakes, and waterfalls. Arrive at the Columbia Icefields Discovery Center and ride the Athabasca Glacier on the Ice Explorer Ride. Later, take a coach from the Discovery Center to the Glacier Skywalk, a glass-floored viewing platform with an unrestricted view of the surrounding glaciers that overlooks the Sunwapta Valley. Continue to the Crowfoot Glacier, Peyto Lake, and Bow Lake along the Parkway. On the way to Lake Louise. Overnight in a motel in Lake Louise.
