Canada Honeymoon Package

Overview

Tour Plan

Day 1: Gala East Coast USA

Hello and welcome to New York. You must approach the Supershuttle desk at your terminal/ground transportation counter or one of the agents for directions upon arrival. If pre-booked, private pick-up is also available for an extra fee. Book a room at Hotel Le Parker Meridian (Check in time 3 PM). Please be aware that the hotel may charge your credit card for incidentals when you check in. If no charges are made, the hold on the card will be lifted when the visitors check out. The pricing does not include porterage. To begin the tour, meet your tour manager in the hotel lobby at 7 p.m. At an Indian restaurant, have a delicious buffet dinner (walking distance). After supper, spend some time at Times Square.

Day 2: Western Canada Rocky Mountaineer - Direct Flights to Vancouver on Air Canada

After breakfast, embark on a full-day tour of Victoria City, including a 90-minute ferry ride through the Gulf Islands on BC Ferries. Upon arrival, travel to Butchart Gardens, where you will be greeted by stunning gardens. Later, visit the Fairmont Empress Hotel, British Import Ships, Parliament Buildings, and the famed Inner Harbour. Return to Vancouver by boat in the evening. Overnight in a motel in Vancouver.

Day 3: Jasper

Proceed to the Explore Jasper Tour after breakfast. The beauty and history of Jasper are placed against some of the Rockies' most jagged peaks. The town of Jasper, Athabasca Viewpoint, Maligne Canyon, and Maligne Lake are all must-sees. Stroll down the 50-meter walls of Maligne Canyon and learn about the canyon's unique subsurface water system. Afternoon free to participate in extra activities such as White Water Rafting, Jasper Skytram, Maligne Lake Cruise, Sky Gazing, and more. Overnight in a motel in Jasperct 04865 231587. Working Time9am to 3pm.

Day 4: Jasper to Lake Louise via Columbia Icefields

After breakfast, check out and make your journey to Lake Louise via the Icefields Parkway, Canada's most spectacular mountain highway with views of glaciers, lakes, and waterfalls. Arrive at the Columbia Icefields Discovery Center and ride the Athabasca Glacier on the Ice Explorer Ride. Later, take a coach from the Discovery Center to the Glacier Skywalk, a glass-floored viewing platform with an unrestricted view of the surrounding glaciers that overlooks the Sunwapta Valley. Continue to the Crowfoot Glacier, Peyto Lake, and Bow Lake along the Parkway. On the way to Lake Louise. Overnight in a motel in Lake Louise.

Day 5: Lake Louise to Banff

After breakfast, check out and make your journey to Lake Louise via the Icefields Parkway, Canada's most spectacular mountain highway with views of glaciers, lakes, and waterfalls. Arrive at the Columbia Icefields Discovery Center and ride the Athabasca Glacier on the Ice Explorer Ride. Later, take a coach from the Discovery Center to the Glacier Skywalk, a glass-floored viewing platform with an unrestricted view of the surrounding glaciers that overlooks the Sunwapta Valley. Continue to the Crowfoot Glacier, Peyto Lake, and Bow Lake along the Parkway. On the way to Lake Louise. Overnight in a motel in Lake Louise.

Day 6: Departure

After breakfast, check out and make your journey to Lake Louise via the Icefields Parkway, Canada's most spectacular mountain highway with views of glaciers, lakes, and waterfalls. Arrive at the Columbia Icefields Discovery Center and ride the Athabasca Glacier on the Ice Explorer Ride.
